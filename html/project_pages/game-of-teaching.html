ICIA - Game of Teaching

ROLE:
Web Developer & Designer

TOOLS:
GatsbyJS, Contentful, HTML, Sass, Illustrator


2019

Working for Illinois Classrooms In Action, I was tasked with designing and developing a website for teachers to explore topics to help with their teaching.

I was asked to design the website as a board game, so I made an interactive spin wheel and card shuffle animation with HTML & CSS. The background was made in Adobe Illustrator.
